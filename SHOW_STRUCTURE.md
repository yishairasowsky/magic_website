# Yishai — Show Structure & Repertoire

## Full Repertoire

| # | Effect | Type | Description | Reaction Level |
|---|--------|------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | Cryptext | Stage | Borrowed bill, multiplied numbers, poster reveals matching decimal + client's name | TOP 3 — Killer closer |
| 2 | PK Touch | Stage/Close-up | Touch one person, another feels it with eyes closed — exact location, timing, strength | TOP 3 — Deeply personal |
| 3 | Name and Place | Close-up/Stage | Two spectators think of famous people, billet switch/burn, dual revelation | TOP 3 — Impossible feel |
| 4 | White Dwarf | Close-up/Stage | Spectator writes anything on a ball, sealed in envelopes, divined via slit peek | Versatile, strong |
| 5 | Ultimate Vacation | Close-up | Impromptu — spectators call out info, double writing, instant sealed prediction reveal | Quick and punchy |
| 6 | Magic Square | Stage | 4x4 grid where every combination adds to spectator's chosen number | Great reaction, mathematical wow |
| 7 | Invisible Deck / Brainwave | Stage/Close-up | Thought-of card is the only one reversed in a jumbo pack | Classic opener |
| 8 | ACAAN | Stage | Thought-of card at thought-of number in a mem deck on the table | Strong follow-up to Magic Square |
| 9 | Book Test | Stage | Spectator chooses page/line/word, audience shouts letters, anagram reveals the word | Interactive, theatrical |
| 10 | Phone Unlock | Close-up | Break into spectator's locked phone | Devastating, feels real |
| 11 | Drawing Duplication | Close-up/Stage | Can be combined with Name and Place | Visual |

## Sequences (Effects That Chain Together)

### The Card/Number Sequence (15-20 min)
1. Invisible Deck / Brainwave — thought-of card revealed as reversed
2. Magic Square — thought-of number, grid filled live
3. ACAAN — same card found at same number in mem deck

### Name and Place + Drawing Duplication (10-12 min)
1. Two spectators think of famous people
2. Billet switch, burn, dual revelation
3. Drawing duplication woven in

---

## Show Formats

### A. Stage Show (45 min)

**Opening (0-10 min) — Establish credibility, hook the room**
- Ultimate Vacation (quick, impromptu, instant impact)
- PK Touch (visceral, gets the whole room watching)

**Middle (10-30 min) — Build intensity**
- Name and Place + Drawing Duplication (personal, impossible)
- Book Test (interactive, involves multiple audience members)
- Card/Number Sequence: Invisible Deck → Magic Square → ACAAN (mathematical, escalating)

**Closer (30-45 min) — Leave them speechless**
- Phone Unlock (transition from intellectual to "wait, this is REAL")
- Cryptext (ultimate closer — multiple reveals, personal to client, standing ovation moment)

### B. Walk-Around / Restaurant (2-4 min per table)

**Set 1:** White Dwarf (anything written, divined)
**Set 2:** Ultimate Vacation (quick prediction)
**Set 3:** PK Touch (two people at the table)
**Set 4:** Phone Unlock (if the vibe is right)

Rotate sets so nearby tables don't see the same thing.

### C. Corporate Reception / Networking (roaming, 3-5 min per group)

- Ultimate Vacation
- White Dwarf
- Phone Unlock
- PK Touch

Short, punchy, conversation-starting pieces. No props that slow you down.

---

## Notes

- Cryptext is ALWAYS the closer for stage shows — it's the most theatrical, most personal, most impossible
- PK Touch works in any context but hits hardest with a crowd watching
- Phone Unlock is a reputation-maker but use with discretion — read the room
- Magic Square → ACAAN sequence needs the audience warmed up first, don't open with it
- For restaurant work: White Dwarf and Ultimate Vacation are your workhorses — quick, clean, no table space needed
