Herzliya -- and especially Herzliya Pituach -- is home to some of Israel's most prestigious corporate offices, luxury hotels, and high-end residential areas. Events here tend to reflect that: well-produced, international in feel, with guests who have high expectations and the experience to know when something is genuinely good.

I'm Yishai Rasowsky -- a professional magician and mentalist performing across Israel. I work in English and Hebrew and I perform at everything from intimate private gatherings to large corporate events at Herzliya's hotels and conference venues. Here's what I do and how it fits.

Why Close-Up Magic Works So Well Here

Corporate events and upscale private parties in Herzliya Pituach share something in common: the guests are accomplished, educated, and a little hard to impress. A stage show with a microphone at the front of the room is the wrong format. Close-up magic -- performed directly in the guests' hands, one group at a time -- is the right one. It's personal. It's surprising. And when something impossible happens right in front of you, with your own cards, your own coins, your own thoughts -- there's no explaining it away.

Mentalism in particular works extremely well with this audience. The more someone thinks they understand how things work, the more powerful a genuine mentalism moment becomes.
