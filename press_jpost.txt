Magician from Beitar Illit Volunteers at IDF Base -- Soldiers Call It an Act of Brotherhood

Yishai Rasowsky performs back-to-back shows for 1875 Yehuda Battalion at Maccabim Camp

Jerusalem, June 2026 -- When Yishai Rasowsky arrived at Maccabim Camp to perform for reserve soldiers of the 1875 Yehuda Battalion, the soldiers were surprised by more than his magic. Rasowsky, a professional close-up magician and mentalist, had traveled from Beitar Illit -- a predominantly Haredi city where military service is uncommon -- to spend an evening entertaining the men entirely as a volunteer.

The soldiers noticed. For families in the battalion who send sons and fathers into uniform while watching others their age remain exempt, the gesture carried weight well beyond the performance itself. Several soldiers told Rasowsky afterward that the act of solidarity -- a man from their community choosing to come to them -- meant as much as the show. At a time when religious-secular tensions in Israel are running especially high over the question of military service, the soldiers described the visit as an act of warmth and brotherhood.

"It was very impressive and engaging," said Y.S., an officer of the Dorsei HaMidbar Company. "The audience was truly surprised by every segment. Well done on a very professional show." He added: "Every segment, he managed to surprise the audience and leave us with our mouths open."

After finishing with one company, Rasowsky traveled to a neighboring base the same evening to perform a second show. The performances featured his close-up magic and mentalism in an intimate seated format, drawing the audience in from the first effect.

Reserve soldier L.B. wrote in a public review: "Yishai came to our reserve company fully as a volunteer. It was very enjoyable. Yishai is very professional and manages to surprise every single time."

It was not Rasowsky's first time performing for IDF soldiers. "It was an honor to come share a little fun with the dedicated soldiers," he said. "I wanted to show up for them."
