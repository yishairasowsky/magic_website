Stage Show

45–60 min  ·  Any size audience  ·  Hebrew or English

Close-Up & Strolling Magic

About Yishai

Magic & Mind Reading That Connects

Yishai has been amazing audiences across Israel for years — from intimate family celebrations and sheva berachos to large corporate galas and community events.

His performances combine genuine mentalism, sleight-of-hand magic, and natural humor in a way that feels personal and surprising rather than staged. Every audience is different, and Yishai tailors each show to the occasion.

He has performed at bar mitzvahs, fundraisers, theater festivals, mental health centers, schools, camps, restaurants, and everything in between — bringing that same warmth and professionalism to every room he walks into.
