What Yishai offers

Choose Your Experience

Every performance is tailored to your event — your guests, your atmosphere, your occasion.

🎭

Stage Show

A full interactive performance blending mentalism, magic, and sharp improvisational humor. Audiences participate, laugh, and are left genuinely baffled. Suitable for every age and background.

45–60 min  ·  Any size audience  ·  Hebrew or English
🃏

Close-Up & Strolling Magic

Yishai moves table-to-table creating intimate moments of wonder. Cards, coins, rubber bands — impossible things happen right in your guests' hands. Perfect for cocktail hours and dinner events.

1–3 hours  ·  Any event size  ·  Ideal for mingling
💻

Virtual Zoom Show

Mind-blowing mentalism and interactive magic that works perfectly online. Cards are chosen, minds are read — entirely through a screen. A genuine surprise for remote teams and online events.

45–60 min  ·  Any time zone  ·  Remote teams welcome
🎩
Learn the craft

Private Magic Lessons via Zoom

Want to learn real magic? Yishai teaches one-on-one via Zoom — sleight of hand, card magic, mentalism principles, and performance technique. Sessions are tailored to your level, whether you're picking up a deck for the first time or looking to sharpen a specific skill.

Lessons are available in Hebrew or English, for any age and any skill level. No experience needed — just curiosity.

40-minute sessions  ·  Hebrew or English  ·  Any skill level  ·  Any time zone

About Yishai

Magic & Mind Reading That Connects

Yishai has been amazing audiences across Israel for years — from intimate family celebrations and sheva berachos to large corporate galas and community events.

His performances combine genuine mentalism, sleight-of-hand magic, and natural humor in a way that feels personal and surprising rather than staged. Every audience is different, and Yishai tailors each show to the occasion.

He has performed at bar mitzvahs, fundraisers, theater festivals, mental health centers, schools, camps, restaurants, and everything in between — bringing that same warmth and professionalism to every room he walks into.
