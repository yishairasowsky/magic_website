SCRIPTED SHOW — YISHAI
======================


INTRO
-----
[PROLOGUE]


Welcome + message to host. Warm up crowd — get them to clap and cheer for the host.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


1. OUTSIDE SIGHT
----------------
[PROLOGUE]


Cassidy opener — spectators sight something outside (car / tree). Note who gets both right — use them for B'Wave.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


2. JUMBO B'WAVE
---------------
[PROLOGUE]


Bring back the spectator who got both right in Outside Sight. Jumbo B'Wave cards reveal.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


3. ULTRAMENTAL DECK
-------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator names any card — pass to correct card in memorized deck. "And you could have chosen any card."

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


4. MAGIC SQUARE
---------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator's number (forced XXXIV preshow via Sven pad) — magic square built around their number, totaling it in every direction. Mock-write on poster board for effect.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


5. ACAAN
--------
[PROLOGUE]


Any card at any number. Use the magic square number as the position. Memorized deck.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


6. BOOK TEST
------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator brings their own softcover book. A word is predicted before they open to any page.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


7. WHITE DWARF
--------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator names a famous person. Predicted in a sealed envelope — with a baby gag poster kicker. (1 big envelope, 2 small envelopes — one has slit. Sharpie.)

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


8. TWO-BILLET TEST
------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spec 1: secret code (phone password / ATM pin). Spec 2: something they want (item or place). Billets burned. Code revealed. (Fallback if no iPhone: Q&A — spec writes answer to a question no one else would know.)

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


9. ULTIMATE VACATION
--------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator names: (1) their name, (2) a famous person they want to meet, (3) where they want to meet them, (4) a question they want to ask. All four revealed from a pre-written billet.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


10. PK TOUCH
------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator closes their eyes. Touches to forehead (x1) and nose (x3), plus secret touches. Series of questions — were you touched? how many times? where? same or different?

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


11. CRYPTEXT  [CLOSER]
-----------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Hand out poster. Spectator brings their own 20 shekel bill. Calculator force reveals the serial number was predicted all along.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


OUTRO
-----
[PROLOGUE]


Thank the host. Words of blessing.

[EPILOGUE]
