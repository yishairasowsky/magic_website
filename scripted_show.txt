SCRIPTED SHOW — YISHAI
======================


INTRO
-----
[PROLOGUE]


Welcome + message to host. Warm up crowd — get them to clap and cheer for the host.

[EPILOGUE]

1. KRAUSS OPENER
----------------
[PROLOGUE]


Questions that sit everyone down — last person standing is the one. Divine the famous person they're thinking of. Backup: invisible deck in pocket.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


2. MAGIC SQUARE
---------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator's number (forced XXXIV preshow via Sven pad) — magic square built around their number, totaling it in every direction. Mock-write on poster board for effect.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


3. BOOK TEST
------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator brings their own softcover book. A word is predicted before they open to any page.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


4. WHITE DWARF
--------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator names a famous person. Predicted in a sealed envelope — with a baby gag poster kicker.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


5. 4DT
------
[PROLOGUE]


3-10 spectators each write thought-of info on a billet. Performer divines each one using one-ahead. Billets burned. (Q&A variant: specs write a question instead. Fallback: Two-Billet Test.)

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


6. ULTIMATE VACATION + CLOCK
-----------------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator names: (1) their name, (2) a famous person they want to meet, (3) where they want to meet them, (4) a question they want to ask. Clock trick (Juan Pablo): spec says stop — reveals the hour of the meeting. Full prediction revealed: person + place + question + time.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


7. PK TOUCH
-----------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator closes their eyes. Touches to forehead (x1) and nose (x3), plus secret touches. Series of questions — were you touched? touched a moment ago? how many times? where? same or different?

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


8. CRYPTEXT  [CLOSER]
----------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Hand out poster. Spectator brings their own 20 shekel bill. Calculator force reveals the serial number was predicted all along.

[EPILOGUE]
