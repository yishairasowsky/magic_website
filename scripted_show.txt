SCRIPTED SHOW — YISHAI
======================


INTRO
-----
[PROLOGUE]


Welcome + message to host. Warm up crowd — get them to clap and cheer for the host.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


1. KRAUSS OPENER
----------------
[PROLOGUE]


Questions that sit everyone down — last person standing is the one. Divine the famous person they're thinking of. Backup: invisible deck in pocket. Room applauds while she sits.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


2. MAGIC SQUARE
---------------
[PROLOGUE]


Everyone thinks of a number up to 100. One person called at random shouts theirs out. Magic square built around that number, totaling it in every direction. Fully impromptu.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


3. BOOK TEST
------------
[PROLOGUE]


One spectator brings a softcover book. Others called at random to choose: page, line number, word number. A word predicted before anyone opened to that page.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


4. DRAWING DUPLICATION
----------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Everyone draws something. One person's drawing collected in envelope. Performer reveals the drawing without having seen it. (White Dwarf method.)

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


5. 2BT — TWO BILLET TEST
-------------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Classic Bob Cassidy. Two spectators hand in billets (name + place, or Q&A variant). Both divined.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


6. PK TOUCH
-----------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator closes their eyes. Touches to forehead (x1) and nose (x3), plus secret touches. Series of questions -- were you touched? touched a moment ago? how many times? where? same or different?

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


7. 4DT
------
[PROLOGUE]


3-10 spectators each write thought-of info on a billet. Envelopes numbered (3,1,2 for three / 4,1,2,3 for four etc.) -- judge holds them the whole time. Performer divines each one using one-ahead. Billets burned. (Q&A variant available.)

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


8. ULTIMATE VACATION + CLOCK
-----------------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Spectator names: (1) their name, (2) a famous person they want to meet, (3) where they want to meet them, (4) a question they want to ask. Clock trick (Juan Pablo): spec says stop -- reveals the hour of the meeting. Full prediction revealed: person + place + question + time.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


9. CRYPTEXT  [CLOSER]
----------------------
[PROLOGUE]


Hand out poster. Spectator brings their own 20 shekel bill. Calculator force reveals the serial number was predicted all along.

[EPILOGUE]


——————————————————————————————


OUTRO
-----
[PROLOGUE]


Thank the host. Words of blessing.

[EPILOGUE]
